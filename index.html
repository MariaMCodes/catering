About My Catering Business


Tradition since 2006

My Catering Business was founded over 15 years ago in the inner western Sydney suburb of Burwood by Mr and Mrs Smith. Our mission is to provide unparalleled service to our customers, using the best and freshest ingredients, served by a passionate team of dedicated staff. We only use seasonal ingredients.

My Catering Business aims to bring people together and have a positive impact on our customers, our team, the environment and the community.
